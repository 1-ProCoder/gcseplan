The Anti-Procrastination Code 💪

  • The 2-minute rule: open ONE flashcard or ONE question. Just one. The rest follows.
  • Study starts at a fixed time each day — not "when you feel ready". Action first, motivation follows.
  • Phone in another room during study blocks. Not face-down — another room.
  • You won't feel ready. Start anyway. Grade 9 students feel the same fear — they just start.
  • Missing one day is human. Missing two in a row is a pattern. Break it immediately.
  • Track your streak. Mark every session done. Don't break the chain.
